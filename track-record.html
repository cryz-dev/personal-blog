Before Acta, I Built Content Systems.

I worked with some of Germany's leading agencies, creators and entrepreneurs — not just editing videos, but helping them turn content into structured growth engines.

Over the course of a year, I worked closely with high-level personal brands and agencies in Germany. My role extended into storytelling architecture, retention-focused hooks, repeatable content formats, publishing workflows and strategic positioning inside their niche. The goal was never just a good video. The goal was a system that compounds.

  • Story structures for retention
  • Distribution-driven hooks
  • Repeatable content formats
  • Publishing workflows
  • Strategic positioning

What I Learned

Most creators don't fail because of quality. They fail because of inconsistency and lack of clarity. Talent without structure doesn't scale. This realization became the foundation for building Acta.
